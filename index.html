Borrow or return lab equipment

Use this iPad whenever you take equipment from the lab or bring it back.

01

Before you begin

Four simple steps

  1. 1 Select the equipment group from the large buttons.
  2. 2 In Assetbots, choose Checkout to borrow or Check In to return.
  3. 3 For checkout, find your name. Then scan each item’s QR label or search by asset tag.
  4. 4 Review every item and confirm. Wait for the success message before leaving.

Cannot find the correct person or item? Do not select a similar record. Ask a member of VR Lab staff.
