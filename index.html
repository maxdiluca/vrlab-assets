Before you begin

Four simple steps

  1. 1 Select the equipment group from the buttons on this page.
  2. 2 Sign in with your authorised account if Assetbots requests authentication.
  3. 3 Find the correct asset by scanning its QR label or searching by asset tag.
  4. 4 Check the record carefully before making any permitted check-in, checkout or inventory change.

Cannot find the correct asset or do not have permission? Do not use a similar record or another person’s account. Ask a VR Lab administrator.
